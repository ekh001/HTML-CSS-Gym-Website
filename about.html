Welcome to Just Dance Gym! Just Dance Gym was launched in the year 2022 when owner Elizabeth Hall, having recently moved to the Hot Springs area from Seoul, South Korea, realized that her new world was just too different from where she had been living. Although she's not a dancer herself, Hall discovered how easy it was to hire professional K-Pop dance choreographers to fly from Seoul to Hot Springs, Arkansas! Our professional dance instructors are all too happy to teach K-Pop dance to Arkansans of all ages!

Our studio offers a reasonable 90000 square feet of practice space, state-of-the-art lighting (so you can see your reflection in the sweat dripping off of your forehead), and an opportunity to learn the hottest dances in the K-Pop world!
